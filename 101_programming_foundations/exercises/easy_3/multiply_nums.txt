Create a method that takes two arguments, multiplies them together, and returns 
the result.

Example:

multiply(5, 3) == 15

Understand the problem:
  create a method that receives two numbers and returns the product of those 
  numbers
  
Input
  two numbers
  
Output
  the product of the two numbers
  
Logic:
  create a method that takes two arguments
  return argument1 * argument2
  (assuming no validation)
