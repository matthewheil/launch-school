Print all odd numbers from 1 to 99, inclusive. All numbers should be printed 
on separate lines.

Understand the problem:
  print all odd numbers from 1 to 99 on separate lines
  
Input:
  n/a
  
Output:
  display odd numbers from 1 to 99 on separate lines
  
Logic:
  for number in (1..99)
    next if number.even?
    display number + 1
  end
