Build a program that displays when the user will retire and how many years 
she has to work till retirement.

Understand the problem:
  ask the user for two inputs: their age & age of retirement
  calculate the difference in years and store in a variable
  display the current year and the year + age difference
  display how many years to go

Example:
  What is your age? 30
  At what age would you like to retire? 70
  
  It's 2016. You will retire in 2056.
  You have only 40 years of work to go!
  
Input:
  age
  retirement age
  
Output:
  Difference in age and retirement age
  Current year and year of retirement
  
Logic:
  prompt user for age
  store in variable
  
  prompt user for retirement age
  store in variable
  
  calculate difference in retirement age and age
  store in variable
  
  display current year
  add difference in ages in years to current year and display
  
  display how many years to go (difference in years)
